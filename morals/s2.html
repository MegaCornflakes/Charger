Pre-Conventional: Stage Two

Stage Two is self-interest driven. A person in this stage may ask what they get out of doing the task. If nothing, then they shouldn't do the task. Example: A child is asked to do a chore, but he doesn't do it because he doesn't get anything out of it. His parents say they will give him a reward, so he does the chores.
