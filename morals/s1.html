Pre-Conventional: Stage One

Stage One is based on the punishment one is given after doing a task. How wrong the task is directly relates to the severity of the punishment given. Example: A child takes something that is not his. His parents therefore don't allow him to have dessert that night. According to this stage, he should not take things that aren't his to avoid being punished.
