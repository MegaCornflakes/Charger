Conventional: Stage Four

Stage Four is authority driven. This stage determines what is morally wrong by the laws. If a law is broken while doing something, it is morally wrong. Most members of society are in Stage Four. Example: Someone steals from a store, and since it is breaking a law, it is morally wrong.
